Lord of the Rings

The Lord of the Rings, by J.R.R. Tolkien, tells the story of the War of the Ring in the fictional world of Middle-earth.
The long novel--commonly published as three volumes and mistakenly called a trilogy--centers around the magical One Ring, discovered by Bilbo Baggins in the earlier novel The Hobbit.
Now, the free peoples of Middle-earth, such as Men, Elves, Dwarves, and Hobbits, must overcome the dark power of Sauron by destroying the Ring.
